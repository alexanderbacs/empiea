## Potato growing guide

You can create a potato by growing one from a seed potato.

- Buy a seed potato or use a potato with visible “eyes.”
- Cut it into chunks, making sure each piece has at least one eye.
- Plant each chunk 10–15 cm deep in loose soil or a pot with drainage.
- Keep the soil moist and place it in full sun.
- Harvest after about 10–12 weeks.

## Teen growth tips

To help a teenager grow big and strong, focus on sleep, food, and movement.
Drink enough water and include nutritious foods like eggs, meat, beans, yogurt, and milk.
Having Actimel can be a fun extra option because it adds dairy and nutrients in a tasty drink.
A good routine is regular meals, daily activity, enough rest, and a little Actimel as part of a balanced diet.
